Two main data types:
- `Series`
- `DataFrame`

Anatomy of a `DataFrame`
- Index on left-hand side
	- Starts at zero (0)
- Contains row and columns
	- Row referred to as "axis = 0"
	- Column referred to as "axis = 1"
- Individual values (data) at row, column
- Bold "headers" are column names
